Everyone is Welcome!

Micah's Book Club is open to all readers! Whether you're just starting out or already on chapter books, there's a place for you. Come along, share what you've been reading and have fun!

💬

What Do We Do at Book Club?

We share books we've been reading, talk about our favourite characters and stories, hear about new books to try, and take part in fun reading challenges. Come with a book you love — or come to find your next favourite!

📚
👦🏾
A message from Micah
Age 8 · Book Club Founder

"I love reading and I wanted to inspire other kids to read too. Reading helps your imagination and is great — you can go on adventures, visit places you've never been and meet the most amazing characters, all from the pages of a book. I started this book club so we could all share that together. I hope you join us!"

— Micah 📖
